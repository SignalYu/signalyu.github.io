2024#

Be cautious in both speech and action.

First things first.

Avoid living a life of suffering.

Opportunity favors the prepared mind.

Focus on your own matters, respect others’ choices, and live in harmony with destiny.

Life is the process of cultivating your soul.

Think first, act later.

Speak less of what’s unnecessary, do less of what’s pointless, and waste less energy on what’s unimportant.

In times of peace, prepare for challenges.

Hope for the best, but always plan for the unexpected.
